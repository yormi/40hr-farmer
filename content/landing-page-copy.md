## PROBLEM — "The grind is real"

**Section heading:** You love what you do. But the hours are breaking you.

**Card 1 — The grind is invisible**
Vegetables sell cheap. Margins are thin. To make ends meet, most growers subsidize their farm with the only thing that's free: their own labor. The culture of "just grind harder" makes the problem invisible — until burnout hits.

**Card 2 — Labor grows faster than sales**
The work multiplies as the farm scales. Revenue doesn't keep up. Most growers end up working 80 hours a week just to stay afloat. You love farming. But the pressure never switches off.

**Card 3 — Knowledge is scattered**
You know something needs to change. The problem isn't motivation. It's that the knowledge to make it happen lives in the heads of a handful of experts, scattered across research papers and hard-won trial and error.

---

## SOLUTION — "The greenhouse multiplier effect"

**Section heading:** Cut labor without cutting sales.
**Intro:** We start where the biggest gains are hiding: your greenhouse. The technical challenges are real, but the upside is massive.

**Card 1 — Your most valuable crop**
Most field beds bring in low hundreds of dollars. A well-run greenhouse bed brings in thousands. Improving your radish crop won't move the needle. Improving your tomatoes changes everything.

**Card 2 — Automate the daily grind**
Rolling up sides at sunrise. Irrigating multiple times a day. Running back to protect crops from cold. In a greenhouse, you can automate most of this — better results, less worry, no lost sales.

**Card 3 — Unlock untapped potential**
The best market gardeners get 5x more tomatoes than the average. Most growers never tap the real potential of their greenhouse because the techniques are highly technical. We're changing that.

**Bottom line:** Same sales. A lot less labor.

## HOW IT WORKS — "It's about not doing it alone for once."

**Section heading:** How it works
**Intro:** The plan works on paper. But farms are messy, seasons are unpredictable, and motivation fades. That's where the community comes in.

**Step 1 — Monthly Challenge**
*One shared goal, everyone moves together.*
Every month the community rallies around the same concrete target. You take action on your farm and share what happens. The results shape the playbook for everyone.

**Step 2 — Weekly Guidance**
*5-minute videos + real obstacle surfacing.*
Short, focused content gives you the knowledge to act. When the community hits real obstacles, we tackle them together.

**Step 3 — Coaching & Support**
*You're never stuck alone.*
- 2-minute check-in with a virtual consultant to track progress
- Monthly live coaching session tailored to your situation
- Community forum — questions answered within the week
- Bonus content based on what the community surfaces
