01 One goal, one month Picked to match what your greenhouse needs. ›

Each month, one goal for everyone in the program. Picked to match what you need to focus on that month. Everyone works the same topic at the same time. That's where the group gets its momentum: questions pile up on the same thing from different farms, and the exchange gets specific fast.

02 Short video Five minutes. Frames the goal. Rewatchable. ›

What to look for in your own greenhouse, the specific moves to try. Phone-watchable between rows.

03 Weekly live call 60 min with Guillaume, Antoine, and Andrew. ›

You bring the obstacles and uncertainties that came up during the week: photos, numbers, questions. We work out your next action to move forward.

Recorded, so missing one is fine. If you join live, bring what you have. Or just listen while you answer emails. You won't be put on the spot.

04 Applied on your farm Pruning, scouting, harvesting, sharpened by the goal. ›

Everything happens inside the work you already planned. The goal sharpens what you watch for, what you decide, what you adjust. Between calls, a community forum: we answer once a week.
